How to Play

The game is pretty straight forward

Once you click the "Start" button, You will then begin! You will be given 15 questions — one after the other. All of which will be asking you to identify one thing... Who does the poo belong to?


Each question will have a different picture of a poo belonging to an aninimal in particular, and beneath will be 4 options to choose from (4 different animal names). Only one option will be correct.


Your final score will be provided once you have completed all 15.
